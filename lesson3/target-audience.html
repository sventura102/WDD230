Target Audience

My target audience for this website are for the average person to be able to have access to know what the weather is like in any case that they need it.

Personas

Persona 1

Steve is the average man that lives in a city, often communting to work by walking there, and needs to know what the weather is like so he can prepare for it.

Persona 2

Janelle lives in the country and grows different fruit on her farm to sell to the city. She needs to know the weather, air quality, and whether or not there will be rain so she can plan accordingly for her farm.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

In the case that Johnny, who lives in New York City, is trying to decide whether or not to wear a jacket today. He checks the weather app out of convenience because he lives in a twelve story building.

Scenario 2

Samantha works at a hospital in Seattle and tends to work between 12 to 48 hour shifts. She hardly knows when it's day time, night time, or what the weather is like outside. She checks the weather app so she knows to put on rain boots when it's raining outside. It rains often.

Scenario 3

Daisy is a grandmother that lives with her two grandchildren that are in elementary school. It snows very often where they live so she needs to know if it's safe to drive normally or if she needs to enable her spike tires in order to drive them to school safely.

Scenario 4

David lives in an area where the Air Quality Index can be very high sometimes, but it's convenient for him to live in that location due to his job, and unfortunately he's someone who is a little more sensitive to air quality changes. On a day where it is high, he has to take his medication so that it doesn't severly affect him.

Scenario 5

Jahzion lives in a city that can get extremely hot, up to 100 degrees, and checks the weather site in any case that he needs to wear lighter clothing.
